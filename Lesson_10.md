# Lesson 10 (Elite Track)

## Program Stage
- Year: 10 (accelerated stream)
- Term: 1
- Week: 4
- Session: Lesson 10
- Duration: 100 minutes

## Lesson Title
Introductory Differentiation and Rate Interpretation

## Lesson Purpose
Launch calculus readiness with derivative-as-rate understanding and polynomial differentiation fluency.

## Learning Intentions
By the end of this lesson, the student will:
1. Interpret gradient as instantaneous rate of change.
2. Differentiate polynomial functions accurately.
3. Use derivatives for stationary points and monotonicity.
4. Communicate derivative conclusions in context.

## Success Criteria
- Core set: `>= 85%`
- Challenge set: `>= 70%`
- Timed section complete with clear derivative notation
- Every stationary-point result includes classification

---

## Minute-by-Minute Flow (100 min)

### 0-10 min: Retrieval Warm-Up
1. Gradient of line through \((1,2),(5,10)\)
2. Expand \((x+2)^2\)
3. Find axis of \(y=x^2-6x+5\)
4. Define rate of change in one sentence
5. Evaluate \(f(3)\) for \(f(x)=x^3\)

### 10-30 min: Explicit Teaching
- Difference quotient idea.
- Power rule \(\dfrac{d}{dx}(x^n)=nx^{n-1}\).
- Linearity and constants.
- Stationary points from \(f'(x)=0\).

### 30-55 min: Guided Core Set
1. Differentiate: \(f(x)=3x^4-5x^2+7\).
2. Differentiate: \(g(x)=\dfrac12x^3-4x+9\).
3. Find stationary points of \(h(x)=x^2-8x+3\).
4. Find intervals where \(p(x)=x^3-3x\) is increasing/decreasing.
5. If \(s(t)=2t^2-5t+1\), find velocity at \(t=3\).
6. Find tangent equation to \(y=x^2+1\) at \(x=2\).

### 55-70 min: Singapore-Style Non-Routine Block
1. Construct polynomial with derivative zero at \(x=1,3\).
2. Explain why linear functions have constant derivative.
3. Compare shape and derivative sign chart for \(x^3-6x^2+9x\).

### 70-85 min: Timed ATAR-Style Mini Test
1. Differentiate \(y=5x^3-2x^2+x-4\).
2. Find stationary points of \(y=x^2+4x+1\).
3. Find tangent to \(y=2x^2-3x\) at \(x=1\).

### 85-100 min: Exit Ticket
1. \(\dfrac{d}{dx}(7x^5)\)
2. Stationary x-value for \(y=x^2-10x\)
3. One sentence: what does negative derivative mean?

## Core Solutions
1. \(f'(x)=12x^3-10x\)
2. \(g'(x)=\dfrac32x^2-4\)
3. \(h'(x)=2x-8=0\Rightarrow x=4\), point \((4,-13)\), minimum
4. \(p'(x)=3x^2-3=3(x-1)(x+1)\): increasing on \(( -\infty,-1)\cup(1,\infty)\), decreasing on \((-1,1)\)
5. \(v(t)=s'(t)=4t-5\Rightarrow v(3)=7\)
6. Slope \(=2x|_{x=2}=4\), point \((2,5)\), tangent \(y-5=4(x-2)\)

## Timed Test Solutions
1. \(y'=15x^2-4x+1\)
2. \(y'=2x+4=0\Rightarrow x=-2\), point \((-2,-3)\), minimum
3. \(y'=4x-3\Rightarrow m=1\) at \(x=1\), point \((1,-1)\), tangent \(y+1=x-1\Rightarrow y=x-2\)

## Exit Ticket Answers
1. \(35x^4\)
2. \(x=5\)
3. Function value decreases as x increases locally.
