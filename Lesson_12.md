# Lesson 12 (Elite Track)

## Program Stage
- Year: 10 (accelerated stream)
- Term: 1
- Week: 4
- Session: Lesson 12
- Duration: 100 minutes

## Lesson Title
Mixed Mastery Assessment and Repair Cycle Launch

## Lesson Purpose
Consolidate first-term accelerated foundations in a mixed-format lesson and trigger targeted repair loops for weak strands.

## Learning Intentions
By the end of this lesson, the student will:
1. Execute mixed-topic problems under timed pressure.
2. Diagnose errors by category (`C`,`P`,`A`,`R`,`T`).
3. Design strand-specific repair actions for the next cycle.
4. Demonstrate improved quality of written mathematical communication.

## Success Criteria
- Core mixed set: `>= 85%`
- Challenge mixed set: `>= 70%`
- Timed section completed on schedule
- Error log complete with at least 6 corrected items

---

## Minute-by-Minute Flow (100 min)

### 0-8 min: Setup and Standards Reset
- Review performance standard and marking bands.
- Confirm notation, checking protocol, and timing targets.

### 8-48 min: Mixed Core Assessment (40 min)
1. Simplify: \(\dfrac{2^{-2}+3^{-1}}{6^{-1}}\)
2. Solve: \(\log_2(x-1)=3\)
3. Find inverse of \(f(x)=\dfrac{x+3}{2}\)
4. Find equation through \((2,-1)\) with slope 4
5. Solve: \(\cos\theta=\dfrac12\), \(0^\circ\le\theta<360^\circ\)
6. Probability: two fair coins, \(P(\text{at least one tail})\)
7. Sequence: find \(u_{12}\) if \(u_n=5+3(n-1)\)
8. Differentiate: \(y=4x^3-7x\)
9. Evaluate: \(\int_0^2(3x+1)dx\)
10. Prove \(n(n+1)\) is always even.

### 48-63 min: Challenge Block (15 min)
1. Solve: \(3^{2x}-10\cdot3^x+9=0\)
2. Find tangent to \(y=x^2-5x+4\) at \(x=3\)
3. Show if \(x+\dfrac1x=5\), then \(x^2+\dfrac1{x^2}=23\)

### 63-78 min: Timed ATAR-Style Sprint (15 min)
1. For \(x^2+(k-1)x+9=0\), find k for real roots.
2. Compute area under \(y=2x\) from 1 to 4.
3. In bag with 4 red, 6 blue, find \(P(\text{red then blue, no replacement})\).

### 78-92 min: Error Audit and Repair Design
- Tag each error by protocol.
- Rewrite full correct method for every error.
- Select three priority strands for next fortnight.

### 92-100 min: Exit Commitment
- Write next-cycle commitments: accuracy target, timing target, writing target.

## Core Solutions
1. \(\dfrac{1/4+1/3}{1/6}=\dfrac{7}{2}\)
2. \(x-1=8\Rightarrow x=9\)
3. \(f^{-1}(x)=2x-3\)
4. \(y+1=4(x-2)\Rightarrow y=4x-9\)
5. \(\theta=60^\circ,300^\circ\)
6. \(1-\dfrac14=\dfrac34\)
7. \(u_{12}=5+33=38\)
8. \(y'=12x^2-7\)
9. \([\dfrac{3x^2}{2}+x]_0^2=8\)
10. Consecutive integers include one even factor.

## Challenge Solutions
1. Let \(u=3^x\): \(u^2-10u+9=0\Rightarrow u=1,9\Rightarrow x=0,2\)
2. Derivative \(2x-5\), slope at 3 is 1, tangent through \((3,-2)\): \(y=x-5\)
3. Square relation to obtain \(x^2+1/x^2=25-2=23\)

## Timed Sprint Solutions
1. \((k-1)^2-36\ge0\Rightarrow k\le-5\) or \(k\ge7\)
2. \(\int_1^4 2x\,dx=[x^2]_1^4=15\)
3. \(\dfrac{4}{10}\cdot\dfrac{6}{9}=\dfrac{4}{15}\)
